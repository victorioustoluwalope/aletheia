The General is a man of many parts- an administrator, a financial analyst, a strategist, a manager of man, material and money. The General is a Member of the Association of Certified National Accountants of Nigeria (CNA), Member of Nigeria Institute of Management (MNIM), member of the prestigious National Institute of Policy and Strategic Studies (NIPSS) where bagged his MNI. He attended the University of Lagos and Federal University of Technology, Akure, and bagged his PhD in Accounting from Marlborough University, Honolulu, USA. Whilst in the Army he had the following medals to his credit: Forces Service Star (FSS), Meritorious Service Star (MSS),Distinguished Service Star (DSS) and United Nation Interim Forces in Lebanon (UNIFIL). Since leaving the army on a meritorious note, he has devoted his time to business and academic pursuits. He has been successful in both and has acquired degrees up to PhD from reputable universities. He attained the summit in the military, business and academic fields through hard work. This was characterized by determination, honesty, integrity and good character. He has decided to impart these ideals in young Nigerians for proper national development. His vision is to build the Nigerian that the Nation clamors for.
